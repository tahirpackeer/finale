Help with the preservation of sea turtle nests!

You can actually help endangered sea turtles survive by making a donation to our hatchery. Your gift will support our monitoring, preservation, and ecological and behavioral study on sea turtles. Together, we can make sure that these amazing animals go on for many more years.

Reasons you would want to donate?

  • It might further assist in nest maintenance and safeguard Sri Lanka's turtle population.
  • How the funds will be used on improving the facility where the turtles are housed.
  • For the benefit of the turtles' living circumstances.
  • Help purchase more incubators and tanks.
